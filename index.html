Objective:

To build prediction model for the stock market of six major technology companies namely Google, Amazon, Facebook, Apple, Tesla and Microsoft.

Data Sources:

  • Yahoo finance library
  • New York Times API (https://developer.nytimes.com/)
  • Reuters (https://www.reuters.com/)
  • Technewsworld (https://www.technewsworld.com/)

Questions:

Does news headlines affect stock prices? Is is possible to predict the drop or rise in the market on news? Is news the only factor affecting the prices? Does the historic prices effect future stock prices?

Methodology:

We performed sentiment analysis using last 6 years of historical stock market data and news headlines/article snippets collected from the data sources. Applying Natural Language Processing(NLP) on the news we have built a classification model using Random Forest Classifier to predict if news on a particular day contributes to a rise or drop in the stock prices. Also, we have trained and built a model using Recurrent Neural Network (RNN) in tensorflow using historical stock prices of the considered companies and compared its accuracy with the NLP model.
